Events of Rotaract Club of Presidency University :


Breast Cancer Awareness and Screening :


Event Date/Day:

8th March,2022


Time:

9:30AM-4:30PM.


GUEST OF THE EVENT :

Guest Mr Rtn Ramana Annavarapu , MS Usha Narayana, Rtn Vikram Samsi, Ann Nilima Samsi, MR Hemant Sharma


ABOUT THE EVENT:

  • Rotaract club and NSS club of Presidency University, along with the Rotary Oasis, Rotary Palmville and SAKRA Hospital are jointly arranging free detection and awareness camp for women who are below the poverty line.
  • A mobile hospital “Pink Express” is organised as below for the benefit of housekeeping staffs.
  • It was big day with all having a Awareness program by the parent rotary members to the housekeeping staffs in MG13 , and had the screening for 42 housekeeping staffs where everyone has got the normal result which is the best part and happiest one with a lot of information which is more useful for the women's.
  • Rotaract club of Presidency University and Rotary club of Bangalore Oasis in Association with People's Trust and Gram panchayath Rajunkunte has successfully completed the first project "Breast Cancer awareness and detection camp " which was held on 14th July,2022 at Sadenahalli .
  • Totally 43 women screened out of 53 registered.
  • All the 43 where tested normal result with a happy face.
