Events of Rotaract Club of Presidency University 2022-2023 :


Celebrate a world of flavors :


Event Date/Day:

7th, September 2022 /Thursday


Time:

12:00 PM


Club name:

Rotaract Club of Presidency University.


Venue:

People’s Trust, sriramanahalli.

ABOUT THE EVENT :


Rotaract club of presidency university on occasion of National Nutrition week presents ‘Celebrate a World of Flavors’ a nutritional talk to Higher Secondary children “Life is a tragedy of nutrition” EAT RIGHT, FUTURE BRIGHT ! This talk happened in the location of People's Trust where we picked up the students from 7 , 8 and 9 th class to give them the knowledge of nutrition to them . Later we gave them a small activity of arranging a plate with nutrients it was so enjoying and memorable .
