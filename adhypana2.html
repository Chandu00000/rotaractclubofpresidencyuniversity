Events of Rotaract Club of Presidency University :


Adhyapna Phase 2 :-


Event Date/Day:

7th December,2021 /Tuesday .


Time :

11AM - 3:30PM


Faculty Coordinators :

Galiveeti Poornima, Computer Science Engineering Department



ABOUT THE EVENT:


  • Knowledge is power. Information is liberating. Education is the premise of progress, in every society, in every family.
  • Rotaract club of Presidency University presents Adhyapna on the occasion of Children's Day phase 2 in collaboration with Theatre Club.
  • It was a astounding event where the orphanage children 7 kids had visited our university three of the club members picked them from the University transport at 11 am and reported to the auditorium where we conducted the cultural activities like dance, singing, standup comedy and specially the skirt performed by the theatre club which is “Naitika Kathegalu” which had lot of information which the kids liked and got few morals.
  • We had a wonderful lunch time where the food was home prepared then following we had a plantation by this kids which was most best thing of the day .
  • It was finally a successful event where the kids had the smiling faces when they left and the happiness gave us the best memories if the day .
