ABOUT THE EVENT :


We Rotaract Club of Presidency University along with the Rotary Oasis in collaboration with People’s Trust have conducted a Blood Donation Camp ,where the old students of People’s Trust school have came forward and donated blood . And people from different location have come forward to donate blood . We are so happy that there are people from very small age from 18 to and very aged persons till around 60 have come forward to donate blood .
