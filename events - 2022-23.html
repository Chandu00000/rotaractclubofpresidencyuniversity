Assam Relief’s Donation :


Event Start Date/Day:

29th July 2022 Friday


Event End Date/Day::

13th August 2022 Saturday


Club name:

Rotaract Club of Presidency University with RD3190


ABOUT THE EVENT :


RD3190 had taken the initiative for a community based project for helping the people who are affected in the Flood occurred in Assam by providing the necessary needs for those people . We heard of this project and we have taken up the initiative to help them. Keeping health is a necessary need for every one in that situation and more people are been suffering from some small diseases which may lead to a bigger one so , From our club we have donated medicines which are useful for the people who are affected and keeping the babies in concern we donated diapers for the babies.

Breast Cancer Awareness and Detection Camp 3.0 :


Event Date/Day:

18th, August 2022 / Thursday


Time:

09:30 AM -02:00 PM


Club name:

Rotaract Club of Presidency University in association with People’s Trust .


Venue:

Chokanahalli, People’s trust adopted village

ABOUT THE EVENT :


We Rotaract Club of Presidency University along with the People’s Trust and SAKRA Hospital jointly arranged free detection and awareness camp for the third time for women who are below the poverty line between the age group of 21 and above. A mobile hospital “Pink Express” is organised as below for the benefit of people in Chokanahalli . It was big day with all having a Awareness program, and had the screening for Totally 47 women screened and registered, where all the 47 has got the normal result which is the best part and happiest one with a lot of information which is more useful for the women.
