Event Date/Day:

Club name:

Rotaract Club of Presidency University.

ABOUT THE EVENT :


This is our Club's fourth installation report. This is one of our largest projects, and everything went very well. We are overjoyed to have participants from various clubs join us. The presence of the Chief Guest enhanced the event. The speeches from the District Club and Parent Rotary members provided us with a wealth of information and motivation.
