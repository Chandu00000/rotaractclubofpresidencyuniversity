Drawing Competition on the Occasion of Ganesh Chaturthi:


Event Date/Day:

2nd, September 2022 /Thursday


Time:

12:00 PM


Club name:

Rotaract Club of Presidency University .


Venue:

Rajankunte Government School

ABOUT THE EVENT :


“Creativity is allowing yourself to make mistakes. Art is knowing which ones to keep.” Rotaract club of Presidency University on Ganesh Chaturthi presents Drawing Competition for primary school children. Where we visited the school and we made the students draw the picture of Ganesh and the results which we got are awesome we are so amazed by their art and we gave them the winning certificates for the winners of the competition .
