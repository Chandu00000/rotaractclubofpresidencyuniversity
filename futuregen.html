ABOUT THE EVENT :


Rotaract Club of Presidency University had conduct an event on occasion of 75th Independence Day .The club had taken a good step to help the civil labours children for a good cause on 16th August 2022. The club is having a team named “Team Nova” where the team is very active and enthusiastic. The team celebrated Independence Day with labours kids and pinned them the flag and gave few goodies like sweets, cake and few essentials. Children were so happy with a huge smiles and excitement on their faces.
