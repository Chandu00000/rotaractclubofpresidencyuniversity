ABOUT THE EVENT :


RD3190 had taken the initiative for a community based project for helping the people who are affected in the Flood occurred in Assam by providing the necessary needs for those people . We heard of this project and we have taken up the initiative to help them. Keeping health is a necessary need for every one in that situation and more people are been suffering from some small diseases which may lead to a bigger one so , From our club we have donated medicines which are useful for the people who are affected and keeping the babies in concern we donated diapers for the babies.
