Events of Rotaract Club of Presidency University :


Mega Clean up Program :


First Program on Cleanliness Drive :-


Event Date/Day:

02nd October 2021/Saturday.


Theme:

Keep Your Surroundings Clean.


Faculty Coordinators :

Galiveeti Poornima, Computer Science Engineering Department



ABOUT THE EVENT:


As we all know that we will be healthy when our surrounding will be clean and hygiene. Rotaract club of Presidency University and Rotary club of Bangalore Oasis Organized to clean a particular area as a part of Swatch Bharath on the day of Gandhi Jayanthi . The place we choose is a hilly area near Rajanukunte where we found lot of plastic and weeds. We gave our full effort and enthusiasm to our work which keeps our surrondings clean and tidy. It was a good experience and a good exposure for all of us. There were such a beautiful moments and memories in this event which stay with us forever.
