Events of Rotaract Club of Presidency University 2022-2023 :


“Health Camp in Raksha Hospital In collaboration with Rotary Bangalore Oasis”:


Event Date/Day:

03rd Nov , 2022


Time:

9:30am - 2:30pm


Club name:

Raksha Hospital in collaboration with Rotary Bangalore oasis .


Venue:

Raksha Hospital

ABOUT THE EVENT :


We Rotaract Club of Presidency University Conducted a “Health Screening Camp” in Raksha Hospital in collaboration with Rotary Bangalore oasis and with the help of Rotary Bangalore Lakeside, People’s Trust, Raksha Hospital and Indian Cancer Society. In the camp women gone through the following tests Bp, Sugar, Physical and Oral Examination Cervical and Breast Cancer Screening Men gone through the following tests Bp, Sugar, Oral Examination. We are so happy that around 120 members have come for the screening test and we have find any patient who in very critical stage.
